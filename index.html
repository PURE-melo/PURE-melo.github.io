Short Bio

I am currently working as a post-doc at the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Perception and Image Understanding, Ministry of Education, Xidian University（led by Prof. Licheng Jiao
). I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University in 2024, advised by Prof. Xiangrong Zhang
, and B.Eng from the School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University in 2019. My research interests include weakly supervised learning, object detection, and remote sensing image analysis.

Recently, I'm working on weakly supervised object detection and semantic segmentation, and hyperspectral image analysis.
