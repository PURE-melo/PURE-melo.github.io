Short Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at Xidian University (XDU), supervised by Prof. Xiangrong Zhang. My research interests include weakly supervised learning, object detection, and remote sensing image analysis.

Recently, I'm working on weakly supervised object detection (WSOD) and weakly supervised semantic segmentation (WSSS).
